Navient Facing Massive Lawsuit from CFPB

By Nolan Clancy



Student loan finance company Navient faced a lawsuit in 2017 from the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau after years of alleged negligence toward borrowers.

Since the suit, the largest student loan company in the world has said that the issues regarding customer negligence have been solved, but data from the 10 states where complaints are filed most often suggests the issues are still widespread over the past two years.
