The Philadelphia 76ers: a Brief History

In one of the country’s great basketball cities, the Philadelphia 76ers have grown to prominence as a storied franchise with a rich history of great players and teams. Basketball Hall of Famers including Julius “Dr. J” Erving, Charles Barkley, Wilt Chamberlain, Allen Iverson and more all called the city and team home for portions of their careers. Boasting one of the best teams in NBA history (1966-67) and some of the worst (1972-73, 2015-16), the story of the franchise spans decades of highs and lows.
